Features.

Track and manage your screen usage to improve productivity and maintain a healthier digital balance

Screen Time Tracking:

Tracks the total time spent on your device each day. It logs all screen usage, including time on apps and browsing.

App Usage Analytics

Provides detailed analytics on how much time is spent on each app. It categorizes usage by social, entertainment.

Usage Patterns

Analyzes your screen time and identifies patterns, such as which apps you use the most and at what times.

Focus Mode

Suggests periods of time to focus on tasks without distractions from apps.

Usage Alerts

CSends push notifications when you’ve exceeded a preset amount of time on specific apps.

Health Suggestions

Offers recommendations to reduce screen time based on your usage patterns.
